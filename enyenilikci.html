    Recycle Packaging

    • What is the Global Recycling Standard (GRS) Certific
      GRS Recycling

    • The Global Recycling Standard (GRS) is a voluntary set of standards for verifying and tracking the content of recycled materials in a product. It addresses social requirements, environmental principles, labeling and chemical components, with traceability applicable to all supply chains. Covering the production, packaging, labeling, processing, trade and distribution of products made from at least 20% recycled materials, GRS sets requirements for party certification of recycled products, environmental and social practices, chemical restriction and chain of custody.

    • GRC Recycle supports organizations that verify chemical, environmental and social practices responsible for the production of recycled content and products.This organization, which is generally associated with textiles, covers all recycled materials, not only in this field.

    • What is the Scope of the GRS Certificate?

    • Chain of Custody: Tracks materials from producer to consumer.

    • Recycled polymer material: 20% recycled materials.

    • Environmental impacts: Examines the environmental impacts that occur during the production of products.

    • Avoiding unethical and dubious sources.

    • Worker Rights: Ensure that all workers are treated fairly and equally and meet ILO-recognized labor standards.

    • Supply Chain: Takes into account risk situations along supply chains to avoid harmful environmental factors that can occur when making improvements to sourcing.
    • Benefits of GRS Certification

    • Provides tracking of recycled materials.

    • Addresses quality issues of recycled products.

    • It makes it easier for customers to make informed decisions.
    • It ensures that the damage caused to the environment and human beings by the production purpose is minimized.

    • Malzemelerin geri dönüştürülmesini ve güvenli bir şekilde işlenmesini sağlar.

    • BKB Packaging, Packaging Solutions has GRS Recycling certificate by considering human health and environmental factors. It serves by considering customer satisfaction, environmental factors and human health.

    D2W-BİO-Degradable Packaging


    • What is D2W certification?

    • The D2W certificate is a certificate given to companies that produce rational, economical and revolutionary plastic productions that produce solutions to prevent environmental problems caused by plastics.
      It is a certificate given to materials produced from polypropylene and polyethylene products in a way that will have no impact within the period determined by the manufacturer.

    • BKB Packaging uses D2W plastic material, which is rapidly disappearing in nature, and produces by taking environmental factors into consideration.

    • BKB Packaging, which prioritizes its environmentalist identity with plastic that can only dissolve in nature as water and mass, produces with a service approach that prioritizes not only environmental factors but also human health and protects and improves customer satisfaction.

    Soft PE (Brighter - Softer)

    • SOFT PE, which we produce from a material that is transparent like OPP when viewed and shiny when touched, but as strong and flexible as Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), is an advantageous and Innovative product for many companies that want to present their products in a brighter and higher quality packaging, as well as offering cost advantages in many sectors using PVC packaging with its elegance and showiness.

    • Our product offers solutions to companies with its shiny and soft texture, flexible structure and durability, and is an environmentally friendly and recyclable product.
